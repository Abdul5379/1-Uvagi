Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most commonly asked questions.

What are the system requirements for UVAGI?

UVAGI requires at minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1050 Ti or AMD RX 560
  • Storage: 75 GB available space
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection

For the best experience, we recommend:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700K or AMD Ryzen 5 3600X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 3060 or AMD RX 6600 XT
  • Storage: 75 GB SSD
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
How do I create an account?

To create a UVAGI account:

  1. Visit our official website at www.uvagi.com
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  3. Fill in your email address, username, and password
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  5. Complete your profile information
  6. Download the game client and log in with your new credentials
Is UVAGI free to play?

UVAGI offers both free and premium options:

The base game is free to play with access to core features and gameplay. We offer a Premium Pass for $9.99/month that includes:

  • Exclusive character customizations
  • Bonus in-game currency
  • Priority server access
  • Early access to new content
  • Ad-free experience

We also offer cosmetic items and non-gameplay affecting items through our in-game store.

How do I report a bug or player?

To report a bug:

  1. In-game, press ESC and select "Report Bug"
  2. Fill out the form with as much detail as possible
  3. Include screenshots or video if available
  4. Submit the report

To report a player:

  1. Click on the player's name in the game
  2. Select "Report Player" from the dropdown menu
  3. Choose the reason for reporting
  4. Provide additional details if necessary
  5. Submit the report

Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action according to our community guidelines.

How do I recover my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page at www.uvagi.com/login
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password
  6. Log in with your new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.
