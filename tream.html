Terms of Service

Welcome to Epic Quest! By entering this realm, you agree to abide by these rules. Failure to comply may result in penalties ranging from temporary suspension to permanent banishment from our kingdom.

1. Account Creation

To begin your adventure, you must:

  • Provide accurate registration information
  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Not share or sell your account
  • Be at least 13 years old to play

2. In-Game Conduct

All players must follow the Code of Honor:

  • No hacking, cheating, or exploiting bugs
  • Respect fellow adventurers - no harassment
  • No inappropriate usernames or guild names
  • Keep chat family-friendly

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy as much as you value your loot. Here's how we handle your information:

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Device information for optimization
  • Purchase history (for digital goods)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve game services
  • For customer support
  • To prevent fraud and cheating
  • For analytics (anonymized where possible)

3. Data Protection

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure - protect your account password!

Refund Policy

All purchases of in-game currency and items are final. However, we may issue refunds in exceptional circumstances at our discretion.

Subscription Services

Premium memberships can be canceled at any time, but no refunds will be issued for the current billing period.

Game Rules

To ensure fair play for all adventurers, these rules are strictly enforced:

1. Prohibited Actions

  • Using third-party software to gain advantage
  • Account sharing or boosting services
  • Real-money trading of in-game items
  • Exploiting bugs instead of reporting them

2. Penalties

Violations may result in:

  • Temporary suspension (first offense)
  • Permanent ban (repeat offenses)
  • Removal of ill-gotten items/currency
  • Legal action for severe violations
